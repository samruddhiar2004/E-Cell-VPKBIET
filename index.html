📖 About Us

The Entrepreneurship Cell at VPKBIET is a student-driven initiative that fosters innovation, creativity, and leadership. We provide a platform where aspiring entrepreneurs can learn, grow, and launch their startups. With mentorship, events, and hands-on experiences, we make the entrepreneurial journey exciting and impactful.

🎯 Mission

  • Provide mentorship and guidance to aspiring student entrepreneurs
  • Encourage real-world learning through startup sprints and innovation events
  • Create an active student community focused on innovation
  • Organize expert talks, workshops, and competitions
  • Support students in turning ideas into scalable startups

📅 Events

  • 🏆 EUREKA! The Flagship Event

    Unleash your inner innovator! Present your groundbreaking concepts, network with industry leaders, and stand a chance to win exciting prizes.

  • 🚀 Startup Sprint: A weekend event to ideate and build MVPs with mentorship and teamwork.
  • 💡 Ideathon: Pitch your raw ideas to experienced mentors and refine them into actionable plans.
  • 👨‍💻 Hackathons: Join forces with peers to solve real-world challenges using code and creativity.
  • 🎙 Speaker Sessions: Listen to inspiring talks by startup founders, innovators, and industry leaders.
  • 🛠 Workshops: Hands-on learning experiences covering design, tech, marketing, and business tools.
